About

Our purpose

  • To increase people’s awareness of their UV dosage and exposure

Problem

  • Sun exposure is an important risk factor for burns, redness and skin cancer
  • People’s knowledge of sun exposure and protection is inadequate

Our solution - Goal

  • Design and build mobile app that measures UV exposure of the user and presents the information in an understandable format
  • Evaluate how our system helps users better estimate their UV exposure
